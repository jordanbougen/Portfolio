about.

Hi. My name is Jordan. I am 18 years old and I am a first year student at the Massey University Wellington campus, studying a Bachelor of Design (with Hons.) majoring in Visual Communication Design. My hobbies include eating, exercising, reading and anything related to art.

I have had a very creative childhood, immersing myself in the arts whenever I could. I was very passionate about drawing and other art forms from a young age, taking interest in the art activities in primary school, take extra-curricular art classes and then continuing on through high school taking Art Painting, Art Design and Art History.
